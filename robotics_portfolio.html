Key Projects & Iterations

Robot V1: The Foundation

Focused on initial chassis stability. Identified structural flaws during high-velocity turns, leading to vital lessons in weight distribution.

Robot V2: The Iteration

Redesigned intake geometry and modified gear ratios. The performance bump was significant, enabling consistent, high-speed field traversal.

Autonomous Subsystem

Developed C++ routines that utilized encoder feedback to perform precision scoring sequences without driver intervention.

Engineering Philosophy

  • Design for Serviceability: Building with the expectation of failure to allow for rapid in-match repairs.
  • Embracing Iterative Failure: Viewing early prototypes as critical data collection phases rather than setbacks.
  • Cross-Disciplinary Harmony: Acknowledging that mechanical and software systems must function as a unified architecture.

Team Leadership

As Lead Builder/Captain, I mentored members in fabrication, managed timelines for national competitions, and refined match strategy with drive teams.
